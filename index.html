Our Story

Fresh Bakes,
Good Company

"A place where good coffee and genuine warmth are the only agenda."

Nestled at the edge of Edinburgh's Western Harbour Promenade, Blue Harbour Café was built around a simple idea, that the best part of any day is slowing down with something truly good. Whether that's a flat white that tastes exactly right, a warm croissant straight from the oven, or a glass of wine as the harbour light fades.

We source carefully, bake daily, and pour every cup with the same intention. Come for the coffee, stay for the view, bring the dog.
