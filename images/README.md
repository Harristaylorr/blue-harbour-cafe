# Image Assets Needed

Save the following images into this folder, named exactly as listed:

## Hero
- `hero-bg.jpg` — Best atmospheric shot (the espresso machine steam shot, or interior blue wall shot works great)

## About Section (3 images)
- `about-main.jpg` — Tall portrait photo (coffee + dog, or interior)
- `about-2.jpg` — Square/landscape (croissant + cappuccino)
- `about-3.jpg` — Square/landscape (any café detail)

## Experience Section
- `experience.jpg` — Wide atmospheric shot (matcha latte on marble table, or wine/cheese board)

## Gallery (6 images)
- `gallery-1.jpg` through `gallery-6.jpg` — Any 6 strong Instagram shots

## Instagram Section (6 thumbnails)
- `ig-1.jpg` through `ig-6.jpg` — Latest 6 Instagram posts (can reuse gallery images)

## Tips
- Use your existing Instagram photos — they're perfect quality
- Crop to roughly 1:1 or 4:3 for best results
- Aim for 800–1200px wide, JPG compressed to ~200kb each
